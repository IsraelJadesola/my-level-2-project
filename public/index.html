3. Coordinate Pickup

Securely connect to finalize the pickup and share the food.

Our Commitment to Zero Waste

Millions of pounds of food go to waste daily while communities face scarcity. Eazy Food Share is a local solution, utilizing technology to redirect perfectly good, unused food to where it's needed most, building stronger, more resilient neighborhoods.
